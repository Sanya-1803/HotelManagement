Why Become A Member?

Becoming a member gives you so much more than just a stay at the hotel. It not only gives you an access to our exclusive services but also gives you a chance to become a part of our community. From special discount offers to exciting experiences, you have it all once you become a member.

The benefits you get once you become a member ~

Membership Card:

You get your own personalised membership card which makes it easier for you to get reservations and access to our exclusive services at our hotels all over the world.

Reservations & Services

We have special reservations for our members along with complementary food & room services.

Discounts:

Although we have various discount offers on special occasions, becoming a member gives you a chance to get more exciting discounts at anytime of the year.

Earn Points for Free Stays:

The more often you visit or stay at our hotel, the more points you earn. And earning more points to certain targets can get you a chance to have a free stay at our hotel.

Events:

Becoming a member gives you an opportunity to get VIP invitations for the events that are organised at our hotels.

Fun Activities:

Being a part of our community comes with lots of fun and exciting trips, activities and so much more.
